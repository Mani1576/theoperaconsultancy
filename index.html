What We Do?

We provide experts who create great value for visa categories.

STUDY VISA

Applying for a study visa in countries like Australia, Canada or the UK can be difficult without proper guidance. Thankfully, there are special services available to help with the process. The Opera Consultancy connects people to expert visa consultants.

VISITOR VISA

We specialize in providing Visitor Visas for Australia and Canada to students who wish to invite their family or parents. We strive to make the application process as simple and streamlined as possible, while ensuring each application gets our full attention.

PTE COACHING

The Opera Consultancy is proud to provide PTE Academic coaching that is expertly taught by fully certified teachers who have been endorsed by Pearson staff. To make sure that students receive the same real exam training, their classrooms are equipped with modern technology.

OPEN WORK-PERMIT/LMIA

We also deal with open work permits for Canada, for those whose spouses are in Canada on a study or work permit and want to apply for a dependent open work permit. Additionally, we provide job offers/LMIA for Canada work permits from India at very reasonable costs.

SCHOLARSHIP GUIDENCE

We find the best universities for our students, with the most convenient scholarships. Countries like Australia and Canada encourage foreign students to pursue education in their universities, and provide numerous scholarships to international students.

CAREER COUNSELLING

Here at The Opera Consultancy, we are committed to providing students with an enriching global experience and a platform for demonstrating their knowledge. Our staff help students develop the career paths they want by providing valuable support.
